Apache Libcloud - Terminal

Libcloud REPL is Python shell for standard web-browsers, written in Python,
JavaScript, CSS and HTML5. It comprises of shell, Python REPL,
and brings its own dummy implemetation of Apache libcloud package.

For an overview of implemented commands have a look at the
complete > libcloud-REPL-Reference;

The keyboard accepts the US-ASCII character set.

As key-mapping depends from your browser, you may have to use the cursor
and backspace buttons at the lower right of the terminal. A complete keyboard can be
accessed at the lower left.


Compatibility: Netscape 4+, MS IE 4+ and DOM-aware browsers.
